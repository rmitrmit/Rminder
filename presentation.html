Project Description

Overview

All students face the problems of organizing and committing to their studying, especially in such a period where processes required for learning, attending classes, taking exams, etc. are becoming more and more digitized and complex. There is a Team link for class meetings, hidden in one's friend group's chat box or conversations with one's lecturer; Lesson files are dispersed (and incomplete) on your local machine and nowhere to be found on the learning platform. These, frustrating the students, hampering efficient studying, demotivating them in the long run, and stopping them from their potential already, are only the tip of the “workflow” iceberg.

Since we are one of those students, who strive to do our best in class but are blocked by these conveniences, we wish to change the game. We want every student to wake up energized and excited to believe that today's learning is going to go well, to know that they have a genie on their side who will assist them with every tedious detail of cleaning up the pipeline of academic endeavors: all the scheduling, recalling which classes to take, or when and where to head to what - we want them gone.

What will this help look like, packed as it is into one single product? Though the functionality of a deliverable may scale infinitely, the mainframe should include: (1) integration and automation of multiple platforms used by students and their institutions, (2) tailored reminders for all types of activity, and (3) automated intuitive organization of academic and digital resources.

For our first function, we automate for our users tasks that they have to do frequently, like sending a weekly reminder to their team on important deadlines. We get rid of tedious intermediary steps of opening, closing software, and remembering information in-between actions to accomplish the tasks: no more opening a calendar app to manually add a section, remember another, and firing the email app, looking for receivers, and drafting content, etc. Chains of triggers that link different applications together can save the users time previously spent on much mindless toying and energy previously invested in non-crucial work.

The second function allows our users to receive emails, notifications, or even phone calls that inform them on the next step of their day. Although this may require some little setting up, our application can learn the patterns of receptions and repeat them in the future or ask for its self-adjustment from time to time. This function, based on the logics of Machine Learning (ML) and Artificial Intelligence (AI), has the same root as the third.

Our third function allows our users to step away from the task of organizing files and folders, which can be time-consuming, ineffective, and downright tedious. Based on the acquired insights from our users, we can create a workspace architecture that is clean and intuitive for them to easily access at any time.

Our distinct values are twofold: the focus on students and the incorporation of ML and AI. Though popular integrated and automated workspaces such as Zapier and ClickUp are widely used in corporate environments, they may be challenging to learn for students, as they require familiarization with a diverse range of other productivity software. Moreover, these workspaces can potentially conflict with ones chosen for a particular institution or in idiosyncratic circumstances, which may be due to the lack of a unified one-for-all solution in academic settings. For example, a student may have to use both Zoom and Microsoft Teams, Outlook and Gmail, or Zalo and Canvas, all at the same time, due to different requirements of the lecturers. This can trickle into the incompatibility of those workspaces and applications used, which becomes counterproductive at the end of the day. Our product, therefore, strives to solve these problems for students. Moreover, the workspaces have not yet launched the functionality of automated files and folders organization fueled by AI and ML, which renders our product more useful in terms of functionality.

If we were to work on this project, we would be launchers of one amongst a sea of productivity applications used by all types of users, but still one shining gem in technologies utilized by academics specifically. Following the surge of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence recently, we want to incorporate the functions mentioned above into our product as the unique selling point. In the eyes of recruiters, we would be problems scourgers and action-takers, which are roles required to aid the functioning of any systems in any specializations. Different as we are regarding our backgrounds and goals, and nonetheless collaborating seamlessly, we will not only come up as the dream team collectively, but also decent group players individually, who get the balance of 'forcibly influencing' and 'being swayed' optimal. In short, we have practiced to be good meta-players in a meta-team.

Detailed Description

Aims

Our aim is to develop software to help students learn at their own convenience and in their own way. Mr. Long once said that the advent of the internet made information more widely available, while the coming advent of AI - which we are currently seeing - will filter and personalize the vast sea of information that’s available on the internet and around us to further help us gain relevant knowledge and increase productivity. We could utilize current AI technologies to analyze and deeply understand each student’s learning ability, as well as their aspirations and goals, and guide them through their academic journey with the endless library of data from the Internet, and intelligence of an AI system.

Plans and Progress

We have designed a prototype that incorporated these following elements:

1. Database Implementation: We established a simple yet effective database using Firebase. This database stores crucial information related to lectures, such as date, time, meeting links, and lecture slides. This information is organized and grouped into notifications, making it easier to manage.

2. Front-End Development: Our front-end interface, constructed using HTML and JavaScript, plays a pivotal role in streamlining the user experience. It presents notifications in an organized manner, resembling discrete "blocks." Each block encapsulates the essential details of a lecture.

3. Simplified Email Automation: Within the interface, we introduced a user-friendly feature—a button. When clicked, this button initiates the creation of precomposed emails. These emails are meticulously structured with the correct information and recipients, significantly reducing the user's manual effort. All that remains is to send these emails, ensuring timely communication and access to essential lecture materials.

Through the integration of database management, front-end design, and email automation, we are poised to streamline the educational experience and reduce the friction associated with online learning. This marks a significant stride toward our broader vision of utilizing AI to personalize and optimize the learning journey for all Students.

For future developments, we will adopt Scrum methodology [1]. The methodology requires the following roles: product owner – who sets the vision for what the final product looks like and how to achieve that vision; scrum master – who thoroughly trains other members on Scrum agile workflow and ensures that Scrum agile principles are met throughout all stages; and developers – who contribute directly to the concretization of the product, to collaborate in a transparent and flexible manner in sprints (processes of achieving some defined goals). The methodology also calls for backbone objects such as the product backlog (specifications of use cases and scopes) and the sprint backlog (plans for development stages) upon which the collaborative efforts are based.

Scope and Limits

Our project’s scope is to provide and support a method that can enhance productivity and reduce the time not only for students but also for lecturers and educational institutions. Below are the main functions that our product will conduct:

1. Integration and automation: As our primary purpose of this project, our production will combine and suggest ever single tools and apps that students and lecturers are required to have for different classes. As a result, it will create a common and friendly environment that will optimize the workflows and reduce the manual actions.

2. Tailored reminder: As there are different kinds of events and actions in education environments, our product will support lecturers, students and schools to custom and access to diverse communication gates and time management tools. Consequently, not only lecturers can remind and notify their students, but also students can manage their own groups’ timeframe and divide tasks in group projects.

3. Automation in organizing files and folders: Rminder acts as a repository for files needed for school and work, just like Google Drive and OneDrive. What makes the repository of Rminder superior compared to other cloud workspaces is the ability to automatically arrange these files and folders based on the preferences of the end-users using AI.

Although there are many convenient features in this product, nothing is escape from limitation. It is important to be aware of several restrictions and factors that could have an impact on using experience of this product:

1. User Behavior: It requires time for users to use this software proficiently. As there are many customizable functions, the user needs to learn how to adjust and manage automation tasks. As a result, instruction such as video or training courses should be conducted before using the software.

2. Information Dependency: As we need a tremendous amount of information from users, accessing users' information such as Gmail, timetable, personal information etc. is an ultimate requirement. Consequently, we can easily receive notifications and work proficiently.

3. AI learning: To enhance AI features, it needs a certain amount of time to help the AI detect user behaviors so that we need as much user behavior as possible to develop AI features.

4. Privacy and Security: Due to the deep access to personal information and data, it increases the concern about privacy and security. Consequently, Ensuring about the protection of data is the most essential part that we focus on.
